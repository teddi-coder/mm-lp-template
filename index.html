Frequently asked questions

How do I book a service?

Use the booking form on this page or call us directly on (02) 9000 0000. We’ll confirm your booking and let you know estimated timing when you get in touch.

Do you offer log book servicing that won’t void my warranty?

Yes. We are a licensed MTA-approved repairer and use manufacturer-approved parts and fluids. Under Australian Consumer Law, you are not required to use a dealership for log book services to maintain your new car warranty.

How long does a standard service take?

A standard log book service typically takes 1.5–2.5 hours depending on the vehicle and what’s included. We’ll give you a time estimate when you book so you can plan around it.

Do you give a quote before starting work?

Always. We will never proceed with repairs without your approval. We quote first, you decide — no surprises on your invoice.

Can you service any make or model?

Yes. Our technicians are experienced across all makes and models — Australian, European, Japanese, Korean, and American vehicles. If you’re not sure, just call and ask.

Is there a warranty on your work?

Yes. All parts and labour come with a 12-month warranty. If something isn’t right after we’ve worked on your vehicle, bring it back and we’ll fix it at no charge.

Do you have EFTPOS and can I pay by card?

Yes. We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as cash. Payment is collected on the day your vehicle is picked up.
